Please note that NPV calculations for socio-economic scenario considers twice the revenues that are generated by selling excess heat. This is due to the assumption that selling excess heat adds value to excess heat providers(sources). Similarly, sinks also get value by buying that excess heat. Therefore, revenues generated by excess heat selling are counted twice. This makes NPV calculations under socio-economic scenario optimistic.
